<a></a>: a hyper link that makes the link underlined 

Attribute: gives a link to a website or video

Ex.:<a href="https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/geforce/">website</a>

<h1></h1>: makes text larger and puts on a seperate line.

Attribute: none

Ex.:<h1>Maxwell Dorrington</h1>

<img></img>: puts an image inside Notepad++ 

Attribute: brings an image into your website

Ex.:<img src="babyyoda.jpg">

<li></li>: turns the line into a list

Attribute:none

Ex.:<li>Stepping on Lego</li> 

<ol></ol>: makes your list using (123)

Attribute:none

Ex:<ol>

<li>game lagging</li> 
</ol>

<p></p>: seperates the paragraph from everything else.

Attribute:none
